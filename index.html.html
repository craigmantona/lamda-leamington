About Me

I'm Helen, a Mum of 2 from Leamington Spa. With a foundation built on real industry experience, I bring a practical, insightful approach to drama coaching and LAMDA training. As a former professional actor, I have worked extensively across stage, screen, and voiceover, giving me a well-rounded understanding of performance in all its forms.

My coaching focuses on developing confident, expressive performers while nurturing each individual’s unique voice. Whether preparing students for LAMDA examinations or supporting personal growth in acting, I combine technical precision with creativity, ensuring sessions are both structured and inspiring.

I am passionate about helping students build not only strong performance skills but also self-belief, communication, and presence, qualities that extend far beyond the stage.
